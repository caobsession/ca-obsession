IMPORTANT NOTICE
If you experience issues such as missing buttons, broken progress tracking, incorrect rendering, or unstable behavior, please use the "Reset App Data" option from Settings before starting.

This rebuilds the local app database and resolves most startup-related issues.

Before resetting, always export your backup if you already have study data saved.
📦 YOUR DATA — IMPORTANT INFORMATION

CA Obsession stores all your study data locally inside your browser — on this device only. There is no cloud sync, no account system, and no online backup. Your data remains completely private and offline.

🚀 QUICK START — RECOMMENDED
  • Enable Persistent Storage
  • Export weekly backups
  • Use links instead of large uploads
  • Avoid Incognito / Private mode
  • Use one main browser/device

These 5 habits protect nearly all long-term study data risks.

📤 Export backups regularly

Go to Settings → Export Backup and save the file outside your browser — Google Drive, email, USB drive, or external storage. This backup file is your primary safety layer.

🔒 Enable Persistent Storage

Go to Settings → Storage → Protect. This requests that your browser preserves local study data even when device storage becomes low.

🚫 Avoid clearing browser data

Clearing cookies, site storage, cached data, or browser history for this app may permanently erase your progress, notes, tasks, local attachments, and resources.

🚫 Avoid Incognito / Private Browsing

Private browsing sessions do not preserve long-term study data. Always use a regular browser window.

💻 Laptop or Desktop browsers are strongly recommended

Mobile browsers can behave aggressively during low-storage situations and may clear site data unexpectedly. For the most stable long-term experience, Chrome, Edge, Brave, or Firefox on a laptop/desktop browser is strongly recommended.

⚠️ Important Backup Limitation

CA Obsession uses browser-based local storage systems. Structured study data usually restores correctly from backups, but locally uploaded attachments may not fully transfer across different devices or browsers.

For maximum reliability: use links for large study resources, export backups regularly, and primarily use one stable browser/device.

If the app behaves unexpectedly: Settings → Reset App Data — but always export a fresh backup first.
